I am a research fellow at [Robert Bosch Centre for DS and AI (RBCDSAI)](https://rbcdsai.iitm.ac.in/) at [Indian Institute of Technology Madras](https://www.iitm.ac.in/), working under the guidance of [Prof. Balaraman Ravindran](http://www.cse.iitm.ac.in/~ravi/). I am currently exploring hierarchical reinforcement learning and graph machine learning. Previously, I worked as a software engineer at [Sprinklr](https://www.sprinklr.com/). Before that, I graduated from [Indian Institute of Technology Bombay](https://www.iitb.ac.in/) in 2022 where I pursued dual degree with master's in AI and Data Science associated with [CMInDS](https://www.minds.iitb.ac.in/), bachelor's in Mechanical Engineering and a minor in Computer Science.
